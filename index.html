Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Moments

Nestled in the peaceful greenery of Budhanilkantha, New Valley Banquet has been Kathmandu's most trusted event venue for over a decade. We believe every celebration deserves elegance, warmth, and meticulous attention to detail.

From intimate family gatherings to grand wedding receptions, our team of dedicated professionals ensures your event unfolds flawlessly — leaving you free to cherish every precious moment.

Full in-house catering with Nepali & continental menus
State-of-the-art audio-visual and lighting systems
Dedicated event coordination team
Ample parking for all guests
Scenic Budhanilkantha hillside setting
